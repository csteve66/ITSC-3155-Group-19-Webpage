Team vision

Our Mission Statement

We are a team of computer scientists who build with purpose, collaborate with respect, and continuously learn from each other. Our mission is to create clean, accessible, and innovative software while growing as developers and supporting one another's success.

Team Rules

  • Communicate daily in our group chat.
  • Respect every idea during code reviews and meetings.
  • Meet all sprint deadlines and if you're stuck, ask for help early.
  • Each member makes at least one meaningful commit per feature.
  • Test your changes locally before pushing to the main branch.
  • Keep our shared GitHub repository clean by useing branches and pulling requests.
